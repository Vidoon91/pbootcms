Why Your Project Needs This Solution

In complex power applications, customers are not only choosing products, but managing long-term system risks.

  • Polluted and coastal environments increase flashover risk
  • Heavy rain, icing and strong wind affect insulation and mechanical performance
  • Long-term outdoor operation requires lower maintenance and longer service life
  • Higher voltage levels require stable and reliable insulation design

Our Solution

WishPower provides complete insulation solutions from application analysis to product configuration, helping projects operate reliably over the long term.

  1. Application Systems: transmission lines, distribution networks, substations and renewable energy projects
  2. Core Products: composite insulators, surge arresters, fuse cutouts and insulation bushings
  3. Technical Goals: insulation protection, overvoltage protection, mechanical support and long-term reliability
